About Yiru


我叫Yiru，專長從事UI&UX 介面設計與使用者經驗研究，擅長使用 Sketch、Photoshop、Ilustrator與各種flow流程與wireframing技能，具備多次專案合作經驗與處理問題能力。

從事UI&UX介面設計工作經驗約三年，熟悉網頁Mockup製作設計與手機APP介面製作流程。亦曾從事過易用性研究、介面架構設計、APP行為互動流程編寫、介面草圖與原型繪製的製作經驗。目前外包經驗約為1年以內，主要接洽網頁UI與APP UI設計，希望有機會能與之合作，謝謝!

Engaged in user experience and visual design, I’m good at Photoshop, Illustrator, Sketch or other prototyping tool to build a variety of flow processes and functional spec to RD. Working in related UI/UX company about 3 years, I have learned how to solve the implemented problem from several project cooperation experiences and carry out the real product. Currently I am looking for the freelancer or part-time job of the UI projects. Latest works will be updated in this website.
